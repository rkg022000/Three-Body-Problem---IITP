Project information

  • Topic: Three Body Problem
  • Bodies Involved: Sun 1, Sun 2, Sun 3.
  • Aim: Predict the trajectory of the three particles based on the set of initial positions and momenta.

Interface

To see the animated motion of the trajectory of Three Bodies, we first need to click the Run button besides the Trinket icon in the Animation Section. The Animation Window appears on the screen. The Three bodies, i.e. Sun 1, Sun 2 and Sun 3 , The Play / Pause button , Restart button , Initial Conditions Drop Down, and Animation Speed Slider are visible on the window.
